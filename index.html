About the System

Developed specifically for Brgy. Novaliches Proper, this system aims to modernize how we approach infrastructure and development. By providing a data-driven approach to project evaluation, we ensure that every partnership contributes meaningfully to the community's growth and prosperity.

The predictive tool utilizes key local data points to generate a comprehensive viability score, helping decision-makers and investors make informed choices that benefit both the public and private sectors.
